FAQS

What is MV Hacks?
MV Hacks is an annual student run high school hackathon hosted by students of Monta Vista High School in Cupertino, California. Our goal is to spread awareness about coding, computer science, and technology.
Who can participate?
Any high school student can participate.
What does this cost?
MV Hacks is free for all participants. All costs are covered.
What does this cost?
MV Hacks is free for all participants. All costs are covered.
I can't code. Can I still come?
If you can’t code, don’t worry! Learning is a big part of the Hackathon experience! We will have programming workshops and lots of mentors to answer any questions that you might have.
How big are the teams?
Teams can have upto four members. Don't worry if you don't have a team, we will have a Team Mixer, where you will meet tons of new people!
What should I bring?
All attendees are required to bring a valid student ID for admission. You should also bring your laptop, phone, and chargers.
